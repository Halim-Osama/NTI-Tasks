Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment with a doctor? -
Yes, simply visit our website and log in or create an account. Search for a doctor based on specialization, location, or availability & confirm your booking.
Can I request a specific doctor when booking my appointment? +
Yes, you can choose a specific doctor during the booking process if they are available.
What should I do if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment? +
You can log into your account and cancel or reschedule your appointment from the dashboard.
What if I'm running late for my appointment? +
If you’re running late, please notify the clinic in advance. The doctor may adjust your slot accordingly.
Can I book appointments for family members or dependents? +
Yes, you can book appointments for your family members under your account.
